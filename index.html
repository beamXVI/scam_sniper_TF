What is Scam Sniper?

Scam Sniper is an AI-powered system that detects and prevents online scams like phishing websites, fake messages, and AI-generated frauds. Designed to protect beginners, elderly users, and rural internet users.

🔍 How It Works

  1. You enter a suspicious link or message.
  2. Our System performs IP tracking, domain checks, and AI-based scam pattern detection.
  3. Scam is flagged with a warning and fraud risk level.
  4. Future versions: real-time alerts via browser plugin or mobile app.
